Launching Anti-Theft functions through Web Management

The required device management actions may vary by region of operation.

To launch Anti-Theft functions through Web Management:

  1. Open Web Management on any device.
  2. Sign in to Web Management with your Kaspersky Account that you used during initial configuration of the app.
  3. Select the device for which you want to be able to remotely launch the Anti-Theft function.
  4. In the sections in the upper part of Web Management window, selection the actions to be performed on the device.
