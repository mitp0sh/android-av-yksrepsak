SIM Watch

SIM Watch is available if a SIM card is inserted in the device.

You can allow or block the SIM Watch function that lets you obtain the new phone number of the device after the SIM card has been replaced to enable launching Anti-Theft functions using SMS commands. You can also allow or block automatic locking of the device if the SIM card is replaced or the device is switched on without it.

In the Ways to receive new phone number and device location section of the SIM Watch settings, you can specify the phone number and email address to which the new phone number will be sent when the SIM card is replaced. The Device lock section also lets you enable locking the device when the SIM card is replaced or the device is switched on without a SIM card.

If the function is allowed on the device, when the SIM card is replaced or the device is switched on without it, the app performs the selected operation (for example, sends the new phone number via SMS or email and locks the device).

To send an email message, the app sends an SMS to the dedicated number of the MTS mobile carrier (Russia). The MTS mobile carrier (Russia) sends the new phone number to your email address. SMS messages to the special number are billed at the rate of current mobile operator.

If the function is blocked on the device, the app does not control actions on the SIM card.
