Disabling data hiding

Privacy Protection is available only with the premium version of Kaspersky Internet Security on devices with an inserted SIM card.

You have to disable data hiding to manage confidential contacts (for example, view calls and text messages from confidential contacts) and configure Privacy Protection settings.

To disable data hiding:

  1. On the quick launch panel in the main window of Kaspersky Internet Security, tap Privacy Protection.
  2. Toggle the Privacy Protection switch to disabled.
  3. Enter the secret code.

    If you forget the secret code, you can recover it.

Information about confidential contacts is displayed.
