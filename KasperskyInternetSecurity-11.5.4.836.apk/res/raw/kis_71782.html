About Scanner (Anti-Virus)

Scanner (Anti-Virus) detects and neutralizes threats on your device.

The component is called Scanner in the free version and Anti-Virus in the premium version.

Scanner performs the following functions:

  • Scan. Launches on-demand scanning of the entire file system, installed apps only, or a selected file or folder.
  • Update. Downloads up-to-date anti-virus databases used to detect threats. Updates ensure up-to-date protection of the device.
  • Quarantine. Stores detected threats in a contained way.

Anti-Virus supports the full Scanner functionality while also providing real-time protection. To provide real-time protection, Anti-Virus uses anti-virus databases and the Kaspersky Security Network cloud service.
