Mugshot

You can remotely allow or block the Mugshot function that lets you use the website to obtain a photograph of the person who is currently using your device, as well as lock your device.

Mugshot only works on devices with a front camera, and doesn't work on devices without a front camera.

The Mugshot feature lets you get a photo of the person currently using your device. This function secretly turns on the front camera, takes a photo of the user, and locks the device.

If this function is allowed on the device, after the app receives the Mugshot command, it secretly takes a photo using the front camera and then locks the device.

If the function is blocked on the device, the app cannot execute the Mugshot command.
