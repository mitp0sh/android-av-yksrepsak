Widget

The widget color indicator and image on the Home screen informs you about the protection status of your device and allows you to configure the app settings.

A green shield indicates that the recommended or extended protection mode is enabled. A gray shield indicates that Protection is disabled. An exclamation mark in a yellow triangle means that the license has expired.

You can allow/block displaying of the widget on the Home screen of the device.

Enable widget

Allow / block the display of the widget on the Home screen of the device. If the check box is selected, the widget is displayed in the list of available widgets. If this check box is cleared, on restarting the device, the widget is not displayed in the list of available widgets and cannot be added to the Home screen of the device.
