About Quarantine

The app moves files detected during scans of the device or during the operation of Protection to Quarantine (a dedicated isolated vault). The app stores quarantined files in packed form so they cannot harm the device.

You can view files moved to Quarantine. You can also delete or restore files using the context menu.

When a file is deleted from Quarantine, it cannot be restored. When a file is restored from Quarantine, the app moves it to the original folder from which the app removed it.
