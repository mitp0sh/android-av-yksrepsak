Web Management

If your device is stolen or lost, you have the option of using Web Management to lock or locate the device, wipe data from it, and get the mugshot of the person currently using your device. To do so, log into the Web Management using your account that you specified during initial configuration of the app.

Synchronization

During synchronization, the app receives commands and sends information about their execution to the Web Management.

It is necessary to have an Internet connection established on the device for synchronization.

The expected time for synchronization depends on the app version.

The device is linked to Kaspersky Account

Kaspersky Account lets you manage Anti-Theft features on your device via Web Management.

Kaspersky Account is a single account for accessing all Kaspersky Lab services. If you have a registered My Kaspersky Account or an account with Web Management, this means that you already have a Kaspersky Account.
