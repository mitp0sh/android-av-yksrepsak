Renew the license

Each time the app starts, Kaspersky Internet Security checks for the presence of a premium license for Kaspersky Internet Security.

If you are using the premium version of the app, you can view the number of days remaining before your license expires by viewing your license information.

When your license has less than 14 days left before it expires, you can renew it. To do this, you can do the following:

  • Purchase a new license from an online store:
  • Renew the license via My Kaspersky.

    My Kaspersky is only available for regions of the United States and Great Britain.

  • Enter an activation code you received previously when Purchase a license.

When the license expires, the app automatically switches to the free version.

To renew the license with the activation code:

  1. On the quick launch panel in the main window of Kaspersky Internet Security, tap Settings > Additional Settings > Licensing > Renew.

    The app may prompt you to use a license for your account detected on My Kaspersky. If it does, decline to activate the app using the detected license by tapping Other activation methods.

    My Kaspersky is only available for regions of the United States and Great Britain.

    If the app does not detect a license for your account that can be used to activate the app, skip to the next step.

  2. Select Enter activation code.
  3. Enter activation code, received in advance when purchasing a new license.
