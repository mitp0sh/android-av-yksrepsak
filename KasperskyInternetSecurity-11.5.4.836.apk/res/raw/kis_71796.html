Action on threat detection

Selection of the action that the app performs on detection of a threat. You can select one of the following actions:

  • Quarantine: the app moves the detected file to Quarantine.
  • Delete: the app deletes the detected file.
  • Skip: the app does not perform any actions on the detected file and does not delete it from the device.

Regardless of the selected action, the app notifies you that a threat has been detected.
