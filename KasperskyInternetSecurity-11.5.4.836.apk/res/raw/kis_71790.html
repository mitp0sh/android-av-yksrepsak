About scanning

You can start the following scans:

  • Full scan.

    Kaspersky Internet Security scans the entire file system of the device.

    A full scan helps to protect your personal data and money, as well as detect and eliminate threats on your device (in both installed apps and installation packages).

    The majority of malware threatens the user's personal data on the device; it collects the device owner's personal data and all the information on the device (for example, GPS coordinates and messages) and sends it to a malicious user.

    Kaspersky Lab recommends to scan the device entire file system for viruses at least once after installing Kaspersky Internet Security, to ensure that personal data is protected.

  • Scanning specific files and folders.
  • Quick scan.

    Kaspersky Internet Security scans installed apps only.

    If you are using the free version, Kaspersky Lab recommends running a quick scan every time you install a new app.

You can also configure automatic launch of full scan of the device according to a schedule created in advance.
