Update startup

You can update the anti-virus databases and the app itself from the device or remotely using My Kaspersky.

Updating through My Kaspersky is only available for regions of the United States and Great Britain.

To update the anti-virus databases and the app itself:

On the quick launch panel in the main window of Kaspersky Internet Security, tap Update.

To update the anti-virus databases and the app itself through My Kaspersky:

  1. Open Web Management on any device.
  2. Sign in to Web Management with your Kaspersky Account that you used during initial configuration of the app.
  3. Select the device you want to manage.
  4. Select Kaspersky Internet Security in the list of apps installed on the device.
  5. In the left part of the window, tap the Protection Status button.
  6. In the Anti-Virus block, tap the Update databases button.
