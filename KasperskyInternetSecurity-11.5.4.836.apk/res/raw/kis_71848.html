Lock & Locate

Allows / disallows remote locking and positioning of the device via Web Management or using the SMS command find: <code> (where <code> is the secret code to Kaspersky Internet Security on your device).

Outgoing SMS commands are billed according to the price plan of the other mobile device.

If this function is allowed on the device, on receiving the Locate & Lock command the app locks the device and shows its coordinates on the website. If you send an SMS command, the app sends the device coordinates in the return SMS. The app also sends the device coordinates in an SMS message to the phone number or email address if you specify them in the Anti-Theft settings in the Ways to receive new phone number and device location section.

Outgoing SMS messages are billed according to the price plan of your mobile service provider.

To send an email message, the app sends an SMS to the dedicated number of the MTS mobile carrier (Russia). The MTS mobile carrier (Russia) sends the device coordinates to your email address.

SMS messages to the special number are billed at the rate of your mobile operator.

If the function is blocked on the device, the app cannot execute the Lock & Locate command.

The app determines the device coordinates using GPS or wireless and mobile networks.

To enable the app to determine the device coordinates using GPS, you must enable GPS in device settings. This enables the app to use the GPS module when it receives the Lock & Locate command. If the app cannot get the device coordinates using GPS, it uses wireless and mobile networks to do so.
