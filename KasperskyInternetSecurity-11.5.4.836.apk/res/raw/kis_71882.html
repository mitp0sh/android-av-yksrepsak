Information to hide

Information and events that the app hides for confidential contacts. You can select types of information to be hidden:

  • Contacts. If the check box is selected, the app hides information about confidential contacts in the device phonebook, and does not show their numbers for incoming calls or in call history. If the check box is cleared, the app displays information about confidential contacts in the device phonebook, and shows their numbers for incoming calls or in the call history.
  • Text message history. If the check box is selected, the app hides incoming and forwarded SMS messages that have been read, and SMS drafts related to confidential contacts. If the check box is cleared, the app displays incoming and forwarded SMS messages that have been read, and SMS drafts related to confidential contacts.
  • Incoming text messages. If the check box is selected, the app blocks the signal and notification on receipt of a new SMS message from a confidential contact, and hides the message in the inbox. To view incoming SMS messages for the period of time when Privacy Protection was enabled, disable Privacy Protection. If the check box is cleared, the app does not block the signal and notification on receipt of a new SMS message from a confidential contact, and displays the message in the SMS inbox.
  • Call history. If the check box is selected, the app hides call history for confidential contacts (for example, accepted, outgoing, or missed calls). If the check box is cleared, the app displays the call history for confidential contacts.
  • Incoming calls. If the check box is selected, the app blocks incoming calls from confidential contacts and hides on-screen information about the call. As a result, the caller receives a busy signal. To view incoming calls for the period of time when Privacy Protection was enabled, disable Privacy Protection. The app hides information about skipped calls in the call history if the Call history check box is selected. If the Incoming calls check box is cleared, the app allows incoming calls from confidential contacts.
