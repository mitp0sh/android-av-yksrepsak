Ways to receive new phone number and device location

SIM Watch is available if a SIM card is inserted in the device. These settings apply when SIM Watch is in operation or when the Lock & Locate feature is started by SMS.

You can specify a phone number and email address to which the app will send a message with the new phone number when your SIM card is replaced. After executing the Lock & Locate command, the app also sends the device coordinates to the specified email address.

To send an email message, the app sends an SMS to the dedicated number of the MTS mobile carrier (Russia). The MTS mobile carrier (Russia) sends the new phone number or device coordinates to your email address.

SMS messages to the special number are billed at the rate of your mobile operator.

Phone number

This field indicates the number to which, if the SIM card is replaced, the app will send an SMS with the new phone number. The phone number may begin with a digit or with a "+", and must contain digits only.

Email

This field indicates the email address to which, if the SIM card is replaced, the app will send a message with the new phone number. After executing the Lock & Locate command, the app also sends the device coordinates to the specified email address.
