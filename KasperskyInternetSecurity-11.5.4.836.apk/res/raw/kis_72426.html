Action on threat detection

On detecting a threat in an installed app, Kaspersky Internet Security prompts you to choose an action to be taken on this app (delete or skip).

If Kaspersky Internet Security detects a threat in a file other than an app (such as a text or music file, archive or distribution kit), the app moves this file to Quarantine by default.

You can configure the default action to be taken by the app on threat detection.

You can view information about threats detected in the Status and Reports sections.

To view information about detected threats, perform one of the following operations:

  • In the main window of Kaspersky Internet Security, tap the shield or swipe the screen from right to left to open the Status section.
  • On the quick launch panel in the main window of Kaspersky Internet Security, tap Settings > Additional Settings > Reports.
