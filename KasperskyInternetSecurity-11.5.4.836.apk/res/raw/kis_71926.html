Device lock

SIM Watch is available if a SIM card is inserted in the device. These settings apply when SIM Watch is in operation or when the Lock & Locate feature is started by SMS.

You can enable automatic locking if the SIM card is replaced or the device is switched on without it.

Lock when SIM card is replaced

If the check box is selected, the app locks the device when the SIM card is replaced or the device is switched on without it. To unlock the device, you must enter the app secret code.

If the check box is cleared, the app does not lock the device when the SIM card is replaced or the device is switched on without a SIM card.

Text when locked

Text which is displayed on the screen when the device is locked. A standard message is displayed by default.
