Automatically adding numbers from Contacts to the list of allowed numbers

Call & Text Filter is available only on devices with an inserted SIM card.

In the Allowed contacts and Standard filtering modes, you can allow incoming calls and text messages from numbers in the device phonebook (Contacts). You will no longer need to add such contacts to the allowed contacts list.

To allow calls and text messages from contacts in the device phonebook:

  1. On the quick launch panel in the main window of Kaspersky Internet Security, tap Settings > Call & Text Filter.
  2. Select the Allow Contacts check box.
