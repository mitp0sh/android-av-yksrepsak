Purchase a license

If you choose to use the premium version of Kaspersky Internet Security, you can purchase a license for the app through My Kaspersky, the Kaspersky Lab online store, or from partner companies.

My Kaspersky is only available for regions of the United States and Great Britain.

When Purchase a license you receive an activation code that you must use to activate the premium version of the app.

When Purchase a license through the Google Play Store, the app is activated automatically.

To purchase a license from an online store:

  1. On the quick launch panel in the main window of Kaspersky Internet Security, tap Settings > Additional Settings > Licensing > Activate premium version.

    When renewing your license the button is labeled Renew.

    If a license is detected for your My Kaspersky account that can be used to activate the app, Kaspersky Internet Security prompts you to use it. When the detected license is used, the app is automatically activated.

    My Kaspersky is only available for regions of the United States and Great Britain.

    If you do not want to use the detected license, but would instead prefer to purchase a license through an online store, tap Other activation methods.

  2. Select Purchase a license.

The web page of the online store opens, where you are able to purchase a new license.
